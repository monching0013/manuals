Water Tanks

System Overview

The water tank on the roof of each block is monitored for the following:

  • Water Level Sensor
  • Over flow switch

Alarms

The following alarms would be generated on BMS.

High / Low Level Alarm:Level HIGH and LOW alarms will be generated if the water tank level rises above or falls below the water tank level sensor alarm limits.
Overflow Alarm:This alarm will be generated the flow switch fixed on the overflow pipe is ON
