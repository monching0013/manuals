Smoke Extract Fan

System Overview

BMS will not have any control on the operation of the smoke extract fan. Fire alarm system will have primary control on the start/stop operation of the smoke extract fan system. smoke extract fan will be stopped automatically once the Fire signal return to normal.
BMS shall only monitor the following:

  • Fan HOA Switch Status
  • Fan Air Flow Status/li>
  • Fan Trip status
  • Smoke Extract Damper status

In addition to above Fan Start/Stop command and Smoke Damper open/Close command points are available in BMS system to operate the fan and damper during maintenance purpose. Care should be taken that before starting fan in manual mode its damper is fully opened.

Alarms

The following alarms would be generated on BMS.

Fan Trip:This is generated when the Fan Trip Signal is on as sensed by panel volt free contact.
